Birds: Nature's Marvels

1. Feathers

Birds are the only animals with feathers. Feathers are essential for flight, insulation, and even mating displays.

2. Beaks

Birds have beaks instead of teeth, and their beak shapes are adapted to their feeding habits and environment.

3. Flight

Many birds are capable of flight, thanks to their unique bone structure, powerful muscles, and aerodynamic wings.

4. Migration

Many bird species migrate over long distances to find better climates and food sources, often covering thousands of miles.

5. Song

Birds use songs and calls to communicate, attract mates, and establish territory. Each species has its own unique vocalizations.
